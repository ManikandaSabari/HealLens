FEATURES

Everything You Need,
In One App

From diagnosis to doctor recommendation — HealLens covers your complete health journey.

🔬
AI Disease Scanner

Upload a normal photo or X-ray. Our CNN model detects chest conditions (Pneumonia, TB, COVID), bone issues (Fractures, Arthritis), and skin problems (Infections, Rashes).

🩻
Image → X-Ray Converter

No X-ray machine? No problem. Upload a regular image and HealLens converts it into an X-ray style view using advanced canvas image processing.

🎙️
Voice Input & Output

Speak your symptoms — HealLens listens and responds in your language. Results are also read aloud in Tamil, Hindi, Kannada, or English.

🌿
Ayurvedic Medicine Map

Get personalized Ayurvedic remedies with exact dosages, preparation methods, and a body-based medicine map for every condition detected.

🤖
AI Health Chatbot

Ask follow-up questions about your diagnosis, symptoms, or remedies. The chatbot understands context from your last scan and answers in your preferred language.

📋
Family Health History

One account for the whole family. Track past scans, filter by family member, view health trends, and export full reports as PDF.

🚨
Emergency SOS

If a scan detects a critical condition, HealLens automatically offers to alert your emergency contacts. One-touch SOS calls and shares your health info.

⚡
Severity Assessment

Every scan result is color-coded: Mild ✅, Moderate ⚡, or Critical ⚠️ — with clear guidance on whether to rest at home or visit a doctor immediately.

👨‍⚕️
Doctor Recommendations

Based on your condition, HealLens recommends the right specialist (Pulmonologist, Dermatologist, etc.) and links directly to doctor booking portals.
